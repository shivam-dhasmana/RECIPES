WORLD OF RECIPES

1.PANEEER BUTTER MASALA

Na biryani na chole bhature ka jalwa,
Dil jeet leta hai iska sona sa palwa.
Malai me lipta, masalon me pala,
Ye hai Paneer Butter Masala – sabka dil ka raja bhala.

Khushboo iski door se hi bula le,
Roti, naan, paratha – sabko mila le.
Munh me jaate hi ghul jaye ye pyala,
Wah! Kya kahen Paneer Butter Masala
